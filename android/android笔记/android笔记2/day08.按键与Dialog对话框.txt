1.警示对话框：
1)创建对话框以及对按钮加监听
AlertDialog alertDialog=new AlertDialog.Builder(this)
		.setIcon(R.drawable.ic_launcher)
		.setTitle("警告")
		.setMessage("是否要吃药?")
		.setPositiveButton("是", 监听器)
		.setNegativeButton("不", null)
		.create();
		alertDialog.show();
   alertDialog.dismiss()//对话框消失
